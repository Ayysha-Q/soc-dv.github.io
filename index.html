About Us

SoC-DV is a research group specializing in FPGA, ASIC design, SoCs, Embedded Systems, and more. Our team has extensive experience in delivering innovative solutions in verification, design, and development of complex hardware systems.

Research

Explore our groundbreaking research in SoC design, FPGA architectures, and verification methodologies.

Products and Solutions

We provide custom solutions in FPGA and SoC development, including ASIC design, embedded systems, and high-performance computing.
